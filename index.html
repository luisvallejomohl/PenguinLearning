PLDU usernames

You can pick most usernames as usernames, but pldu1, pldu2 ... pldu9 are reserved. Usernames that start with PLDU are reserved for users which are not really people. They are used for the testing and development of PenguinLearning. PLDU stands for PenguinLearning Development Users. Users that have a PLDU username as username will be cleared of points.

Privacy policy

We care about your privacy. We add a date to the traffic database each time you visit. This is our Metrics tracking system. We use it to calculate traffic, and just that. We do not use any sort of Web Analytics(which is not the same as Metrics), and we do not collect personal information. We do not store what videos you watch However, we do need to store data in persistent storage(which involves cookies). This, however, only includes:

  • Points
  • Username
  • Password
  • User ID
  • Programming projects
That's all. We do NOT store your IP address, and we do NOT display ads or sell your information to third parties. You may delete at any time, and your account's row in our users databases will be permanently deleted. However, your visit timestamps are still logged to the traffic database.
