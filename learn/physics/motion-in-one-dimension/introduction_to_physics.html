Introduction to Physics

What is physics?

Physics is a natural science. It's primary goal is to understand motion, energy and concepts related. Astronomy is also sometimes included in physics. There are many branches of physics. Physics may be one of the oldest subjects, if you consider Astronomy as a branch of physics (and I do).

What prerequisites do you need to learn physics?

Good question. To learn physics, you need a solid understanding of Algebra and an understanding of trigonometry to a basic level. In some derivations, you may need calculus to a basic level.
