Position-time graphs

Our animation of the yellow cube moving is cool, but what if we want to look at the history of the cube's position over time in a single glance? We can use a graph! A position-time graph, or an x - t graph. An x - t graph is simply a line graph with position put on the y axis and time put on the x axis.

Quick test:

The x - t graph below shows the journey of Joe crossing the road (The origin is the edge of the road where Joe started): How many meters had Joe travelled after 5 seconds?
